OUR SPECIALS

Corndogs

Make American-inspired corndogs by coating frankfurter sausages in a cornmeal batter, then deep-frying and serving with ketchup and yellow mustard.

Fish finger hot dogs

A twist on a classic fish finger sandwich the whole family will love. Try lightly salting the cod to firm it up before cooking.

Sweet and sour recipes

Inspired by Chinese takeaway, our sweet and sour recipes include chicken, pork and vegetarian options. Try our healthy version for a family dinner.

OUR STORY

This elegant restaurant dining area exudes sophistication and warmth. The space features round tables dressed in sleek black tablecloth, each meticulously set with fine glassware, white napkins, and polished silverware, reflecting a commitment to detail and luxury.

Soft, ambient lighting creates an intimate atmosphere, complemented by the warm hues of wooden accents and subtle ceiling lighting. Floor-to-ceiling curtains along one side of the room add a sense of privacy and refinement. The overall layout is spacious, allowing for relaxed conversation and movement, making it ideal for upscale dining, special occasions, or corporate gatherings.
